### Daily Scrum (team)

- Short (<5 minutes) meeting each day
- Everyone on team attends
- Everyone remains standing


- Not a report to a manager
- Purpose if for team to self organize
- *No discussion during scrum*. If discussion needed, have follow-up meeting 
- Generally recommended that managers *not* attend Daily Scrum

### Sprint review
- Key idea: inspect and adapt the *product*
- In-depth converstation between team and client
- Includes a demo, along with discussion
- Not a presentation - no slides
- Guideline: no more than 30 minutes should be spent preparing for that review
